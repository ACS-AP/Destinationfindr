Q & A

Commonly asked questions below

  • Where is it located?

    The exact adress is Aguirre, Quepos, Puntarenas.

  • What is the best way to book a trip here?

    It is best to book 6-12 months in advance. Additionally, it is best to use https://www.expedia.com/ to complete the booking.

  • Is it safe?

    Costa Rica is generally a safe place to travel, especially due to signifigant tourism.

  • What is the weather like?

    Temperatures are consistently warm and tropical year round in Costa Rica averaging between 72 and 82 °F (22 and 28 °C). The northwest is hotter in the dry season February through May when there are no rains too cool things off.

  • What is it also known as?

    Costa Rica is relatively inexpensive and it is easy to get around. Most people speak fluent English.
